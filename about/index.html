About the comic

Crawlspace is a short, serialized webcomic about independence, courage, and things that go bump in the night. It is being written primarily as a learning experience, and for personal enjoyment, so both the artistic quality and writing is likely to be very rough at times. You can learn more about the comic's licensing and how to get the source code for this site by clicking the Github link at the top of the page.

Parents and younger readers should know that Crawlspace is a light horror story that will include some mature content, including violence, references to alcohol, and tense or threatening situations. In order to make the comic accessible to as many readers as possible, an optional swearing filter will soon be made available at the top of the page.

Meet the Characters

Katheryn

Katheryn is a college senior who lives alone and has just moved into a new appartment. Meticulous and organized, Katheryn prides herself in being in control of every situation, all the time.

Lars

Lars is a fellow student in Katheryn's grade, and a fellow neighbor and resident "cool kid" in her new appartment complex. He considers Katheryn to be a close friend. The feeling isn't mutual.

Mrs. Dumphry

An elderly woman living in Katheryn's appartment complex with her grandson. Considers Katheryn to be a nice girl that really ought to come over and visit more often.

Travis

Mrs. Dumphry's ten year-old grandson, who has recently become afraid of the dark. Ocassionally forced by his guardian to acknowledge Katheryn and other strangers' existence, despite the fact that all of these people are incredibly booring.
